"You better, you better, you bet," she half sang, half hummed.

"What's that from?"

"The Who."

"Who?"

"Yeah."

"Wait, what?"

"You really don't know?"

"Know what?"

"The Who."

"Who am I supposed to know?"

"How old are you, exactly?"

"Hey!"

The speed dating buzzer went off.

"Who's next", she sighed.
