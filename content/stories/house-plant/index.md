"What's that?"

"Found it in the attic."

"Open it!"

"Huh."

"What is it?"

"A little plant. How can it still be alive?"

"Some greenery around here would be nice. Give it some water!"

"Oh wow! It's thirsty!"

---

Betty knocked again as vines slithered around her ankles from under the door.
